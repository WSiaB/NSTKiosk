Overview

Welcome to the enlarged and improved Sephora Stonebriar located in Stonebriar Centre in Frisco, TX. At Stonebriar Centre you’ll enjoy an elegant and modern setting providing the perfect backdrop for a day of fun. Stonebriar Centre is a shopping and dining destination in an area that offers a vibrant mix of cultural and entertainment attractions. Make the most of your visit and explore a variety of nearby activities and entertainment. Whether you’re looking for something special for the little ones at the area’s only LEGO store or you’re eager to update your wardrobe with the hottest trends, you’ll find it all at Frisco’s premier shopping destination.
