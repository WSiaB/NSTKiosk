Overview

Welcome Sephora Ka Makana Ali’i !  Located at the Center for West O’ ahu, this gorgeous addition to the Center adds to the charm and beauty of Kapolei. KMA will be the third largest regional shopping center in Hawaii. Its history has been at the center of Ka Makana Ali’i, which means “The Royal Gift” in honor of Prince Kuhio and Hawaiian Royalty. All the roads leading to the sub-divisions and into the Ka Makana Ali’i complex property are named for the Ancestors and family members of Prince Kuhio.
