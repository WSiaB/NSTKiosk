Overview

Welcome to Sephora New Sudbury Centre located in New Sudbury Centre, which is the largest shopping mall in Northern Ontario, Canada. It has 110 stores and 562,619 square feet (52,269.0 m2) of retail space spanning 2 stories. With their recent expansion, New Sudbury Centre has regained the title of largest shopping mall in Northern Ontario.
