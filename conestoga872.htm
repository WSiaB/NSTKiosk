Overview

Welcome to Sephora Conestoga located in Waterloo, ON. Conestoga Mall is a regional shopping centre situated directly adjacent to the area’s major arterial highway, offering exceptional access and visibility. They offer 5.5 million annual visitors a range of experiences all under one roof, whether its shopping at 130+ stores and services including dining in a landmark Food Court featuring 14 vendors and seating for 800+; entertainment at Galaxy Cinema, or exploring history at the City of Waterloo Heritage Museum. They pride themselves on providing a premier shopping experience that includes service excellence for both the shoppers and retailers and a commitment to improve the overall impact on the environment in the areas of energy consumption, water conservation, greenhouse gas emissions and waste management.
